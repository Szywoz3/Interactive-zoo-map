lat. Meriones unguiculatus</br>
de. Mongolische Rennratte</br>
ua. Меріонес монгольський</br>

Distribution: Northeastern China, Mongolia, and Russia</br>

Habitat: Dry steppes and semi-deserts</br>

Diet: Grass and cereal seeds, fruits, roots, green parts of plants</br>

Most active at dawn and dusk; however, depending on ambient temperature, it can also be active during the day.

Well adapted to extreme temperature variations (from -40°C to +50°C) and limited water availability.

Lives in small groups in complex burrow systems. The gerbil can dig tunnels up to half a meter below the ground.</br>

Least Concern – LC</br>
