lat. Nycticebus pygmaeus</br>
de. Zwergplumplori</br>

An arboreal primate of the rainforest. It lives solitarily in the treetops.
Its diet includes insects, bird chicks, and fruits.
Females give birth to 1–4 young.</br>

RED LIST:</br>
Endangered – EN</br>
