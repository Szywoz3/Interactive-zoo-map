lat. Heteropteryx dilatata</br>

Stick insects - Insects</br>

Malaya, Madagascar</br>

The largest herbivorous insect with clear sexual dimorphism.
Wingless, green females reach 15 cm in length and a body mass of 17 g.
Males are smaller, differently colored, and have two pairs of wings.
Newly hatched larvae are 3 cm long and feed collectively on oak leaves, and in later stages on rose and blackberry leaves.</br>
