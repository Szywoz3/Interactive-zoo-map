lat. Phyllostomus discolor</br>
de. Kleine Blattnase</br>

A member of the leaf-nosed bat family, which includes 50 genera and 129 species.
It feeds on insects, fruits, and nectar. During the day, it rests in caves and hollow trees.
Leaf-nosed bats do not hibernate.

Like many other species in this family, this bat has the ability to use echolocation.
It lives in large colonies and gives birth to a single offspring.</br>
