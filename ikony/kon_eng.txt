A breed of small horses classified as ponies, originating from the Shetland Islands.

It is one of the smallest pony breeds, with a height at the withers reaching up to 110 cm.

Their characteristic traits include endurance, longevity, stubbornness, and gentleness.
For this reason, they were historically used for work in mines.

Their thick mane, long tail hair, short ears, and dense coat protect them from harsh weather conditions.</br>
