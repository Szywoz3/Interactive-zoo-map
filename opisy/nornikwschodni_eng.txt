lat. Microtus fortis
de. Schilfwühlmaus
ua. Полівка велика

Distribution: Southeast Asia — regions of Mongolia, the Korean Peninsula, and northern and eastern China.

Habitat: Wet meadows within forests, areas around water bodies and streams rich in dense vegetation.

Diet: Mainly leaves, young shoots, and stems of plants; in winter also roots, grass seeds, and bark of trees and shrubs.

It is active regardless of the time of day.

On land, it does not move very quickly or gracefully, but it is an excellent swimmer.

It digs complex, well-developed burrows with side passages, nesting chambers, and multiple exits.
