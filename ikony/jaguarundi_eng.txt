lat. Puma yagouaroundi</br>
de. Jaguarundi</br>

Distribution: South and Central America</br>

Habitat: Rainforests and dense thickets</br>

Diet: Small mammals, amphibians, arthropods, and occasionally fruits</br>

Jaguarundis are not averse to water – they are good swimmers. They are mainly diurnal but can also be active at night. At midday, they visit water sources.

Although these cats are not hunted for their fur, they are seriously threatened by habitat degradation.</br>
