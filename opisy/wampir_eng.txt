lat. Desmodus rotundus</br>
de. Echter Vampir</br>

The common vampire bat feeds exclusively on the blood of mammals.
Its saliva contains a substance that prevents blood from clotting.
During the day, it rests in colonies of up to 2,000 individuals.
After a pregnancy of about 7 months, a single young is born.
It can live up to 20 years.</br>
