lat. Gerbillus campestris</br>
de. Nordafrikanische Rennmaus</br>
ua. Піщанка північноафриканська</br>

Distribution: North Africa – from Morocco to Egypt, Sahara, and Sub-Saharan countries</br>

Habitat: Cultivated fields, palm groves, dunes, grassy slopes, habitats rich in rocks and vegetation</br>

Diet: Grass and shrub leaves, fruits, seeds, insects</br>

Nocturnal; also active at dusk and dawn.

Spends the day sleeping in a burrow it digs in the ground. This is also where it shelters from predators.

Can jump quickly and agilely, which helps in escaping or climbing rocks.</br>

RED LIST:</br>
Least Concern – LC</br>
