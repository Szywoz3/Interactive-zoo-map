lat. Dasyuroides byrnei</br>
de. Doppelkammschwantzbeutelmaus</br>

A ground-dwelling marsupial inhabiting deserts and dry grasslands.
It spends the day in burrows and rock crevices.
Its diet consists of insects, reptiles, and small mammals.
A female gives birth to up to 6 young.</br>

RED LIST:</br>
Vulnerable – VU</br>
