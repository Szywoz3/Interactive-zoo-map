lat. Strix uralensis</br>
de. Habichtskauz</br>
ua. Сова довгохвоста</br>

Distribution: Europe and Asia</br>

Habitat: fields, forests, wetlands</br>

Diet: small rodents, smaller birds</br>

They bury themselves in large and medium groups, at various times of the day.

In periods without dominance (peaceful), their contacts are limited exclusively to castles.

The most frequent digging activity takes place in the morning and afternoon. They keep a well-dense surface and rarely guard other birds.
Chicks drink their parents’ milk.
Thanks to this, they have dense and controlled absorption.</br>

RED LIST:</br>
Least Concern – LC</br>
