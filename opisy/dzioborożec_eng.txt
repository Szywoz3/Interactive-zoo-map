lat. Anthracoceros malayanus</br>
de. Malaienhornvogel</br>
ua. Птах-носоріг чорний</br>

Distribution: Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand.</br>

Habitat: tropical and subtropical lowland forests.</br>

Diet: fruits, insects, and small vertebrates.</br>

During incubation and while feeding the chicks, the male seals the female inside the tree cavity, leaving only a small opening through which he passes food to her and the young.

The black hornbill is one of the main “seed dispersers” of the durian — an exotic fruit famous for its delicious taste and terrible smell.

It has long “eyelashes” on its eyelids, which is rare among birds.

The sexes can be distinguished by the color of the beak: males have white beaks, while females’ are black.
