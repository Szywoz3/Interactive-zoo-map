Cockroaches - Insects</br>

South America</br>

One of the largest winged cockroaches. Body length up to 7 cm. This insect undergoes incomplete metamorphosis.

The larvae are dorsoventrally flattened. They live in the layer of soil humus.

The giant cave cockroach is omnivorous.

When disturbed, it emits a repellent odor.</br>
