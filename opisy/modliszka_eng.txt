Insects</br>

An order of insects including about 2,000 species, living mainly in subtropical climates.
Body length ranges from 10 mm to 16 cm. In Poland, only the European mantis (Mantis religiosa) occurs.
They resemble leaves and twigs, and due to the structure of their front legs, they appear like a praying figure.
They undergo incomplete metamorphosis. During copulation, the female often eats the male.</br>
