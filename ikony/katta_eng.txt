lat. Lemur catta</br>
de. Katta</br>

Distribution: Madagascar</br>

Habitat: dry forests and rocky areas</br>

Diet: leaves, fruits, nectar, and insects</br>

Lemurs live in family groups ranging from 5 to even 20 individuals. They enjoy basking ("sunbathing") in the sun.

The long tail helps the lemur maintain balance while moving and jumping between trees.

Just like in humans, lemurs also have fingerprints on their hands.

Active during the day, but unfortunately seriously endangered due to ongoing degradation of their natural habitat.</br>

Endangered - EN</br>
