lat. Achatina Achatina</br>
eng. Giant African snail</br>

Snails - Mollusks</br>

Africa</br>

The largest land snails in the world.

Body weight can reach up to 0.5 kg.

Shell height up to 20 cm. They are herbivorous.

They inhabit equatorial Africa, where they stay in humid areas.

Spread by humans, they have become a troublesome agricultural pest.</br>
