lat. Bubo bubo</br>
de. Uhu</br>

Distribution: Europe, Asia</br>

Habitat: wild hilly areas</br>

Diet: rodents, hares, rabbits, foxes</br>

The characteristic "ear tufts" act as receivers, capturing sounds from the surroundings that then reach the ear opening.

Like most owls, they can rotate their head up to 270°. It is the largest owl found in Europe.

The structure of their feathers allows for silent flight. The male calls with a double deep hoot, audible from a distance of 1 km.</br>
