lat. Phyllocrania paradoxa</br>

Insects - Mantises</br>

Africa, Madagascar</br>

They grow to about 5–6 cm. Females are slightly larger than males. They can have various colorations: from black through brown and shades of beige to green.
They live in the savanna and hunt mainly flying insects.</br>
