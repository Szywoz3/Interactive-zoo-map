Beetles – Insects</br>

Europe</br>

These beetles have beautiful, metallic green, shiny wing covers.

They are most often seen in May and June on flowers, especially those belonging to the umbellifer family.

The larvae of rose chafers develop in decayed wood.
An exception is the larva of the green rose chafer (Cetonia aurata), which develops in ant nests.</br>
